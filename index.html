About the Forest Keeper

I am a certified pentester for a conglomerate of various major companies, wielding the digital wand to protect and strengthen the walls of technological fortresses.

My craft involves brewing secure code potions and finding weaknesses in existing spells. I specialize in protective magic through HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Three.js, X3DOM, and A-Frame.

When the moon is full, I venture into the realm of 3D visualization and interactive experiences. When it wanes, I focus on security audits and vulnerability assessments.
